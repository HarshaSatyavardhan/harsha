Brief:

We have developed two interfaces called Near and Far to help deaf-blind people to communicate with normal people using their mobile phones.

At present, our app is limited to the English language so after this campaign we want to extend this project to work on multiple languages and we also want to come up with a solution to decrease the difficulty to teach and use morse code for deaf-blindness people..
