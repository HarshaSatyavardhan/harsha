Harsha Satya Vardhan

B.E. Electronics and Communication

I am a final year undergraduate student in Electronics and Communication student from SRM University AP, Amaravati Campus. My interests lie in the fields of Machine Learning and Deep Learning and their applications in the domain of Computer Vision. Currently, I am working as a Junior Research Fellow at IIIT Hyderabad where I am working in the IHUB team on projects related to Healthcare and Artificial Intelligence. Previously,I have worked as a research intern at NIT Calicut, under Dr.Sudeep, where I worked on the problem of Medical Image Enhancement using Deep Neural Networks DST funded project. I have also worked as an undergraduate researcher at Dr. Jatindra Kumar Dash, SRM Univerity AP where I worked on Classification of Interstital Lung Disease using Deep Learning. In my previous experiences, I have worked on challenging problems such as Explanatery ML, Static and Dynamic Face Gesture Recognition,Building apps for Deaf-blind people etc. Apart from this, I am a core member of Next Tech Lab AP.
